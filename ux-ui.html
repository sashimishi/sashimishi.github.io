UA Hub Website Redesign

As an extension of the UA Hub visual identity project, I redesigned the organization's website in Figma to align with the newly developed brand style and logo.

The redesign moved away from the previous visual approach in favor of a cleaner, more professional and minimalist aesthetic — one that feels modern and timeless. Special attention was given to improving site navigation and overall user orientation, making it easier for visitors to find information and understand the organization's mission at a glance.

Pomodoro Timer

This project was developed as part of a Web Development course at university. The result is a classic Pomodoro timer — a productivity tool designed to improve focus and time management through structured work and break intervals. Both the design and the code were developed collaboratively as a team project, combining our skills to deliver a clean and functional web application.

The app also includes a built-in To-Do List, allowing users to plan and track their tasks directly within the timer, and a music player. Additionally, during breaks, users can chat with friends through a room-based system — simply create a room, share the code, and connect.

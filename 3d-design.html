3D Design

Ava the Arachna

This character was created as part of a 3D Design course at university. Ava is a playful and cheerful Arachne — a half-girl, half-spider mythological creature — designed to feel expressive and full of personality.

The project began with concept art illustrated in Adobe Photoshop, which defined Ava's visual style, proportions, and character. The final 3D model was then built and rigged in Blender, bringing the character from a flat illustration into a fully realized three-dimensional form.

DuckDash! Characters

These characters were designed as part of a Designing AR/VR Experiences course at university.
DuckDash! is an augmented reality game where players must catch as many ducks as possible within a time limit and return them to their nest.
I was responsible for the full character design and animation pipeline — from initial concept to the final animated models.
